Privacy-first contact sharing

Share your contact details with one scan

QuickContact QR lets you create a QR code that stores your contact details directly inside the code as a vCard. No profile page, no database storage, and no need for the website to stay online after the QR code is generated.

Create my QR contact card Learn about privacy

Why use QuickContact QR?

Privacy-first

Submitted details are processed temporarily and are not saved in a database by this app.

Works offline later

The QR code contains the contact data itself, so it can still be scanned even if the website is unavailable.

Easy contact saving

Contact details are formatted as a vCard, which many phones and contact apps can recognize.

Download options

Download the QR code as an image or download the contact card as a .vcf file.

How it works

  1. Enter your contact details.
  2. The app validates the form.
  3. Your details are converted into a vCard.
  4. The vCard is encoded directly into a QR code.
  5. You download and share the QR code.
