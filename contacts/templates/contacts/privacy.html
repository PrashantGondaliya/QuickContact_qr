Privacy-first by design

QuickContact QR is designed to generate contact-sharing QR codes without storing submitted personal contact details in a database.

What happens when you create a QR code?

  • You enter your contact details into the form.
  • The app temporarily uses those details to create a vCard.
  • The vCard is encoded directly into a QR code.
  • The generated QR code is returned to your browser.
  • Your submitted contact details are not saved by this app.

Important sharing reminder

The QR code itself contains your contact details. Anyone who scans your QR code may be able to read or save the information inside it.

Only share your generated QR code with people you want to receive your contact information.
